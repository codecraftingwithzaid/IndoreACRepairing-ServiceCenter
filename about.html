About Us

Chasing the vision to provide clean indoor air, Save energy and save the environment.

What is Indore AC Repair Center?

Venturing into a revolutionary approach to redefine the maintenance landscape of air conditioners worldwide, Clean Air Innovations emerges as a beacon of excellence in ensuring pristine indoor air quality, environmental sustainability, and optimal AC performance.

Established in January 2011 and based in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, Clean Air Innovations is driven by a steadfast mission to deliver impeccable indoor air quality, preserve the environment, and conserve electricity. Our comprehensive solutions cater to both B2B and B2C clients, offering cost-effective and time-saving measures that safeguard the environment without any additional burden.

As a pioneering Cleantech startup, Clean Air Innovations envisions addressing critical issues in the HVAC industry on a global scale, such as air conditioner inefficiencies, greenhouse gas emissions, and escalating electricity consumption, which have profoundly impacted both society and the natural world.

At Clean Air Innovations, we undertake the responsibility to enact positive change within the industry by leveraging cutting-edge technology, specialized training programs, and innovative strategies to provide user-friendly end-to-end solutions.

A core focus of our efforts lies in enhancing consumer awareness, recognizing the immense potential for energy savings, cost reduction, and environmental preservation. Our patented technologies and IoT-based solutions, bolstered by a team of seasoned industry experts with over a decade of hands-on experience, propel us forward in our mission.

Throughout our journey, we have achieved significant milestones, including the granting of patents for our groundbreaking sterilization processes, serving a vast clientele exceeding 5000 satisfied customers, launching a mobile application, and actively developing an IoT prototype to solidify our position as a technological leader. Additionally, forging strategic OEM partnerships and onboarding channel partners have further strengthened our industry presence, culminating in a stellar 4.5-star rating on Google Customer Reviews.

Clean Air Innovations stands poised at the forefront of innovation, sustainability, and excellence, reshaping the future of air conditioning maintenance and environmental stewardship worldwide.
